Applications (Offline-Friendly)

  1. Gather documents: transcripts, letters of recommendation, test scores (if required), ID, photo.
  2. Complete the form: verify all fields; use black ink or a typed copy.
  3. Submit by mail or in person: keep a copy for your records.
  4. Track status: note reference numbers and typical processing times.

Accommodation Options

  • Off-campus housing: shared apartments, neighborhood tips, rental basics.
  • Costs and contracts: what's included, deposits, lease duration.
  • Safety and support: campus security, nearby clinics, residence life contacts.

Checklist: confirm housing type, roommates, accessibility needs, and move-in requirements.

Student Life: On Campus vs Off Campus

  • On-campus: clubs, student unions, events, dining plans, study spaces.
  • Off-campus: transit options, nearby amenities, social opportunities.
  • Balancing work and study: part-time jobs, library hours, quiet zones.
  • Wellness and support: counseling, health services, peer mentoring.

Tip: join 1-2 clubs to build community quickly.
